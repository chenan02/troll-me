What does this do?

troll-me is an algorithm that looks through your Facebook profile and suggests things that you can do to troll people. It will generate comments to post on photos, status updates for your profile, and random photos to like from a while ago.

When we suggest comments and statuses, you will be provided with a text field to fill in what you want it to say. We do not pre-fill any of these fields for you.

*Using this algorithm will NOT post anything to your profile, unless you explicitly press any of the post buttons ("Comment!", "Post!", or "Like!".)
